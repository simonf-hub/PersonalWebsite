About Me

Hi, I'm Simon. This website is a place to store some of my past works. Welcome and feel free to look over them.

I started off being a Cognitive System (COGS) student at UBC. Later I discovered my interest in music and audio processing. So I did my final COGS project on music cognition. Becuase becoming a researcher doesn't align with my career goal. I decided to attend Lasalle College for a Diploma of Recording Arts, where I could gain more practical experiences in audio processing.

Music

My interest lands on music production (writing + sound design + arrangement + mixing),mainly for the genres of folk, R&B, and pop. I'm excited for the unknown "chemical reaction" happening in the process of creating. I also enjoy trying out different music styles.
